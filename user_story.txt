User Story:
The landing page or home page will be the main display for the 
entirety of the website with no extra fluff. At the beginning (top) of this page you will see a minimal stylish design
with my name centered in the middle, a brief bio of myself as a software engineer and 4 links to my 1.github, 2.linkedin, 3.email, 4.to-be-determined.
As a box within a box, on the bottom of this box display you will see an overlay of my different tech domains in different cubes, including computer architecture, 
Full-Stack Web Development, Cybersecurity, etc. As you scroll down, you will see my tech stack, with icons for each individual programming language and/or service.
Beneath this, as you scroll down, you will see a projects section. This section will have cards displaying my best projects on a carousel.
Beneath this section, as you scroll down, you will see a "Feel free to connect with me" text on the left and an input section on the right for 
your name, business no. and email address.
